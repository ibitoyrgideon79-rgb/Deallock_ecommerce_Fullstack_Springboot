About Us

DealLock was created to solve a simple but costly problem: losing valuable deals because payment couldn’t be completed fast enough.

In today’s fast-moving declutter sales, auctions, and discounted markets, great opportunities often disappear within minutes. Buyers are ready and willing, but timing and liquidity become barriers. DealLock exists to bridge that gap.

We provide a Deal Securing & Purchase Support Service that allows customers to lock fast-selling items with an upfront payment while completing the balance over time.

Our approach is built on trust, transparency, and controlled execution. We act as a reliable intermediary—securing assets on behalf of our customers under clear terms.

How It Works

STEP 1

Find a Deal

Identify a fast-selling, discounted, auction or declutter item you want to secure.

⬇

STEP 2

Pay to Lock

Send the item link to us on whatsap, We’ll assess the deal to confirm availability, price fairness, saleability and item authenticity. If approved, you’ll make the required upfront payment of the item.

⬇

STEP 3

We Secure the Item

DealLock.ng acts on your behalf to completes the remaining payment, make the purchase and holds the item securely under clear custody terms.

⬇

STEP 4

Complete Your Payment

You pay the remaining balance within the agreed timeframe(Validity Period) while your item remains safely secured.

⬇

STEP 5

Pickup or Delivery

Once full payment is confirmed, the item is released for pickup or delivery.

⬇

STEP 6

Failure to Proceed

DealLock.ng sells the item, and the buyer receives a refund of their initial payment, after deducting holding fees, Transaction and resale costs
